      Programmers Note

      > Tor-rent is a free-range, open source forum created to challenge notions of free speach in the public sphere. This program gives the illusion a normal macintosh UI while secretly monitoring users location, profile, information and Ip address

      > This program, hence the name, uses a stark comparision between the Tor - Onion browser Aka the dark web and the well-known safari browser to discuss issues relating to internet censorship, surveillance and privacy.

      > The choice of UI and design was not without reason. The vintage macintosh screen is meant to challenge the notion of digital surveillance being seen as a completely modern issue as opposed to a societal one.
