Inspiration & Styles

For the design of this site, I was very inspired by the retro UI design trends also because it reminds us of a time when these issues weren't as prevalent. There were a few websites in particular that really inspired the design: Ordinary Holdings & Reading Machines. The website Reading Machines was particularly interesting because of the way they use sound and type to explore the ways we interact with type. While I didn't push it to the extreme it encouraged me to design a forum that was less-convectional.
